2. Scope - What This Policy Covers

This Policy applies to:

  • Your use of the ScrollStreet mobile app, and
  • Any personal data you provide when you contact us directly (for example, by email).

It does not cover how Apple, Google, PostHog, or other third parties handle your data when you use their services. Please refer to their own privacy policies.

3. Summary

For the current version of the App:

  • We do not require an account
  • We do not ask for your name, email, or contact details inside the App
  • We do not track you across apps or websites
  • We do not show ads
  • We use a privacy-focused analytics tool, PostHog, to understand how the App is used and to improve it. We configure it so that we do not use it for targeted advertising or to directly identify you.

The main personal data we may process is:

  • Data about how you use the app (via PostHog)
  • Whatever you choose to send us if you email us or contact us for support.

4. Data We Process

4.1. Data stored locally on your device (game data)

The App stores the following data locally on your device using your device’s native storage (e.g. iOS UserDefaults):

  • Game progress and settings
  • In-app preferences (for example, sound, theme)

Important:

  • This data is stored on your device only.
  • It is not transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not have direct access to this data unless you choose to share it with us (e.g. via a screenshot).

Depending on your jurisdiction, data that lives only on your device may still legally be considered “personal data” because it relates to you. However, we do not see or control it.

4.2. Data you provide when you contact us

If you contact us (for example, by emailing yoavgrinberg1@gmail.com), we will process:

  • Your email address
  • Any information you include in your message (e.g. your name, device type, screenshots)

We use this information only to:

  • Read and respond to your message
  • Provide support and handle your request

Legal basis under GDPR (where applicable): legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) - our interest in communicating with users and running our business.

We do not use your email for newsletters or marketing unless you explicitly ask us to.

4.3. Usage data and analytics

We use PostHog to analyze how users interact with the App and to improve its features, performance, and user experience. Depending on how you use the App and how PostHog is configured, the information collected may include:

  • Usage events: screens you visit, buttons you tap, features you use, frequency and duration of sessions
  • Device and technical information: device model, operating system version, app version, language, time zone, and similar technical metadata
  • Identifiers: a pseudonymous identifier generated by the App or PostHog so that we can understand returning usage without knowing who you are
  • Approximate location: for example, country or region derived from your IP address or device settings (we do not use this to pinpoint your exact address)
  • Diagnostics: performance metrics, errors, and crashes related to your session

We configure PostHog so that:

  • We do not use it for targeted advertising or sharing data with advertising networks
  • We do not knowingly send directly identifying data such as your real name, email address, or phone number from inside the App
  • Data is used in aggregate or pseudonymous form where possible

We use this data to:

  • Understand how users interact with the App
  • Detect bugs, crashes, and UX issues
  • Decide which features to improve, add, or remove
  • Measure the effectiveness of product changes and updates

Legal basis under GDPR (where applicable): legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) - our interest in understanding how the App is used and improving it.

PostHog acts as our data processor for this purpose and processes the data on our behalf in line with our instructions and their own privacy and security commitments.

4.4. Data collected by app stores and your device

When you download or use the App, Apple (and/or Google, if you build Android later) may process:

  • Device identifiers
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Basic usage statistics

This information is processed under their own terms and privacy policies, not ours. We may see aggregated and anonymised statistics in the developer consoles (for example, number of installs, crashes per OS version), but we cannot identify you personally from this data.

5. No Financial or Payment Data

ScrollStreet is a simulator using virtual currency only.

  • We do not collect credit card numbers, bank account details, or other payment information.
  • All real-money purchases (e.g. in-app purchases, subscriptions) are processed by the Apple App Store (and/or Google Play in the future). We do not see your full payment details.

Please refer to the relevant app store’s privacy and payment terms for details.

6. Cookies and Tracking

  • The App does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies for advertising or analytics.
  • We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.
  • We use PostHog analytics (see section 4.3) to collect usage data about how you use the App. This relies on device identifiers and in-app events, not web cookies.

If this ever changes (for example, if we add analytics or ads in the future), we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy, and
  • Update our App Store privacy disclosures accordingly, and
  • Ask for any consent required by law (e.g. GDPR / ePrivacy).

7. Data Retention

  • Local game data on your device: kept until you uninstall the App or delete the data via your device settings. We do not control this storage.
  • Emails and support messages: we keep these for as long as reasonably necessary to handle your request and keep records of our communications, and then delete or anonymise them unless we are required to retain them longer by law (for example, for accounting or legal purposes).
  • Analytics data (PostHog): we keep usage and analytics data for as long as necessary for the purposes described above (improving and maintaining the App), and then delete or anonymise it. In practice, this typically means a retention period of no longer than is reasonably needed to analyze trends and app performance.

You can always ask us to delete emails you have sent us (see “Your Rights” below). For analytics data, because it is pseudonymous and aggregated, it may not always be possible to identify your specific records, but we will assess any request on a case-by-case basis.

8. How We Protect Your Data

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect any personal data we process (for example, email correspondence), including:

  • Limiting access to email and analytics accounts to the app owner and, where applicable, trusted service providers
  • Using reputable email, cloud, and analytics providers with appropriate security measures
  • Using strong authentication and access controls where available

However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your device secure and for deciding what information you send to us.

9. Your Rights (EU / GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), including the Netherlands, you have rights under the GDPR regarding your personal data, including:

  • Right of access - to know what personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification - to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure - to ask us to delete your personal data
  • Right to restriction of processing - to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability - to receive your data in a machine-readable format (where applicable)
  • Right to object - to object to processing based on our legitimate interests

In practice, because we deliberately collect very little personal data, these rights mainly apply to:

  • Emails and other messages you send us
  • Any personal data that might be contained in analytics events (for example, if you have sent us screenshots or descriptions that end up being tied to a specific usage pattern)

To exercise your rights, contact us at yoavgrinberg1@gmail.com.

We may ask you for information to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In the Netherlands, this is the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens.

10. Children’s Privacy

ScrollStreet is not intended for children under 18, and our Terms of Service require users to be adults (or to use the App under the supervision of a parent or guardian, depending on how you set it up).

We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. Because the App does not require accounts or login and we do not collect personal data in-app, the privacy risk for minors is very low.

If you believe that a child has provided personal data to us (for example, via email), please contact us, and we will delete that information.

Where required by law, we take steps to ensure an adequate level of protection for such transfers, for example by:

  • Using reputable providers that implement standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards, and/or
  • Using data centers in jurisdictions recognised as providing an adequate level of protection.
